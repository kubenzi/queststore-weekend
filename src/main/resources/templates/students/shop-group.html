Shop - group items
Here you can propose to your group mates to buy a group item. To do this, enter the amount you want to allocate to start the fundraiser. You can withdraw funds at any time before its completion. Information about the collection status will be available in the 'Group shopping' tab. If the required amount of coins is collected, the item will be automatically used. Information about the purchase can be found in the 'Transactions' tab.
